About Malcolm

Malcolm Lismore is a freelance photographer based on the North West coast of Scotland. His biggest passion in photography is for the natural world. He sells many images of the rugged Scottish landscape, its natural wildlife, and coastal birds. However, he is also available for weddings, portraits, and special events.
